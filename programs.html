What We Do

Our Core Programs

Six transformative programs aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals — designed to empower African youth comprehensively.

01
SDG 4 — Quality Education

IT Training & Digital Awareness

In today's world, digital skills are no longer optional — they are essential. YAN's IT Training program equips African youth with practical digital competencies that open doors to careers, entrepreneurship and global opportunities.

We cover everything from basic computer literacy to advanced digital tools, online safety, and emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence.

Key Activities

  • IT and Digital Skills Training sessions
  • Online Safety and Cybersecurity awareness
  • AI tools and Digital Entrepreneurship workshops
  • 21st Century Skills development
  • Facilitator and trainer coordination
Join IT Training →
02
SDG 4 & SDG 10

Scholarships & Global Opportunities

Access to quality education should not be limited by financial circumstances. YAN's Scholarships department works tirelessly to research, verify and share global opportunities that can transform lives.

From fully-funded scholarships to internships, fellowships and exchange programmes — we ensure our members know what is available and how to apply successfully.

Browse Scholarship Opportunities →

Key Activities

  • Research and verification of global opportunities
  • Scholarship, internship and fellowship sharing
  • Application process support and guidance
  • Success story tracking and celebration
  • Information sessions and webinars
03
SDG 8 — Decent Work

Career Development & Public Speaking

YAN believes every young African deserves the tools to build a fulfilling career. Our Career Development program provides practical, hands-on training in employability skills, professional communication and confidence building.

Key Activities

  • CV writing and motivation letter training
  • Interview preparation and mock interviews
  • Public speaking and confidence sessions
  • Personal branding and networking skills
  • Career guidance and mentorship
Join Career Development →
04
SDG 16 — Strong Institutions

Leadership & 21st Century Skills

Africa needs ethical, visionary and impactful leaders. YAN's Leadership program identifies and nurtures the next generation of African leaders — equipping them with the skills, mindset and networks to lead positive change.

Key Activities

  • Leadership training workshops and retreats
  • Self-leadership and personal development
  • Governance and civic education sessions
  • Youth leadership forums and dialogues
  • Mentorship from established African leaders
Join Leadership Program →
05
SDG 17 — Partnerships

Community Impact & Giving Back

Real development happens in communities. YAN's Community Impact program mobilizes young Africans to serve their communities — creating tangible, lasting change through volunteerism, outreach and collaborative projects.

Key Activities

  • Community outreach and service activities
  • Volunteer mobilization and management
  • Partnerships with local organizations
  • Youth-led community projects
  • Impact documentation and storytelling
Join Community Impact →
06
SDG 3 — Good Health

Mental Health & Wellbeing

Mental health is health. YAN recognizes that young Africans face unique pressures — academic, financial, social and emotional. Our Mental Health program provides a safe space for awareness, peer support and resilience building.

Note: This department provides non-clinical peer support and referral pathways only.

Key Activities

  • Mental health awareness sessions
  • Peer support programs and safe spaces
  • Resilience and wellness workshops
  • Stress management and self-care training
  • Referral pathways to professional support
Join Mental Health Program →
