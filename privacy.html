Privacy Policy

Young Africans Network | Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Young Africans Network ("YAN", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform at youngafricansnetwork.org.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and profile photo
  • Country, department, and role within YAN
  • Learning progress, quiz scores, and assignment submissions
  • Community posts, comments, and interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our community platform
  • To track your learning progress and issue certificates
  • To send important YAN announcements and newsletters
  • To connect you with scholarship and career opportunities

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, located in the africa-south1 region. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile photo. We do not receive your Google password.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data at any time via your profile
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting us
  • Opt out of newsletters at any time

7. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us at: youngafricansn@gmail.com
