Lab Rules & Standards

The Entrepreneurs Lab is a trusted space. We maintain high standards to protect both buyers and sellers across Africa.

  • Must be a verified YAN member to list
  • Must have completed at least one YAN program
  • All listings reviewed by YAN team before going live
  • No misleading descriptions or false claims
  • Buyers contact sellers directly — no platform fees
  • Reviews must be genuine from real interactions
  • YAN reserves the right to remove any listing
