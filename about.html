About Young Africans Network

A pan-African, youth-led, volunteer-driven organization empowering young Africans through education, leadership, and opportunity.

Who We Are

Our Story

Young Africans Network (YAN) was founded in April 2026 in Kenya by a group of passionate young Africans who believed that the continent's greatest resource is its youth — and that with the right support, guidance and opportunities, African youth can change the world.

We are a pan-African, non-partisan, youth-led organization with members across Kenya, Cameroon, Rwanda, Tanzania, South Sudan, Ghana, Nigeria and beyond. Our work spans eight core departments — each focused on a specific area of youth development.

From digital skills training to scholarship access, career development to mental health — YAN is a comprehensive platform that meets young Africans where they are and takes them where they need to go.

We officially launch on 1st June 2026 — and this is only the beginning.

"To empower African youth through education, skills development, mentorship, and access to opportunities that enable them to actively build the future together."

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01
Integrity & Accountability

Upholding honesty, transparency, and responsibility in all actions. We lead by example and hold ourselves to the highest standards.

❤️
Service & Giving Back

Centering our work on impact and contribution to community. We exist to serve — not to be served.

03
Excellence

Striving for the highest standard in all programs and engagements. We do not settle for average — Africa deserves our best.

02
Collaboration

Building together through teamwork, partnerships, and shared purpose. No one builds the future alone.

04
Impact

Measuring success by the real, lasting change we create in lives and communities. Impact over titles, always.

What We Pursue

Our Core Objectives

👑
Leadership Development

Identifying, mentoring and supporting emerging African youth leaders across education, entrepreneurship, governance and social innovation.

💼
Career & Skills Empowerment

Providing career guidance, professional development, digital literacy, entrepreneurship support and networking opportunities.

🏆
Education & Scholarships

Sharing information on scholarships, fellowships, internships and global opportunities to advance educational access.

🌍
Civic Engagement

Encouraging youth involvement in community service, volunteerism and social development projects across Africa.

♀️
Gender Equality & Inclusion

Promoting participation of young women, persons with disabilities, rural youth and marginalized communities.

💚
Mental Health & Wellbeing

Promoting awareness, providing peer support and integrating wellbeing into all YAN programs and activities.

🌱
Environmental Sustainability

Engaging youth in environmental protection, reforestation and sustainable development campaigns across the continent.

🤝
Strategic Partnerships

Building networks with African governments, AU, NGOs, the UN and youth-led organizations globally.

📊
Monitoring & Accountability

Developing robust mechanisms for tracking progress, impact and promoting transparency within the Network.
