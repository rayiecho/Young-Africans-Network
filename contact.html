Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join YAN?
Visit our Join page, fill in the membership form and we will contact you within 48 hours to complete your onboarding and add you to our community.
Is YAN free to join?
Yes! YAN membership is completely free. We are a volunteer-driven organization committed to making our programs accessible to all African youth.
How can organizations partner with YAN?
Send us an email or fill in our contact form with your partnership proposal. All partnerships are governed by formal MoUs outlining roles and responsibilities.
Where is YAN based?
YAN is founded in Kenya with pan-African reach. Our members and activities span across Africa and the diaspora — we are a truly continental network.
How are activities conducted?
Most YAN activities are virtual — via Google Meet, Zoom and WhatsApp — making them accessible to members across different countries and time zones.
When does YAN officially launch?
YAN officially launches on 1st June 2026 with a continental launch event. Join us to be part of history — register through our Join page today!
