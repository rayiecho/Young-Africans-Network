Be Part of the Movement

Join Young Africans Network

Become a member of a growing pan-African community of young leaders, changemakers and opportunity-seekers. Your journey starts here!

Why Join YAN

Member Benefits

🏆
Scholarship Access
Get first access to scholarships, fellowships, internships and global opportunities shared weekly by our dedicated team.
👑
Leadership Development
Participate in leadership training, workshops and mentorship programs designed to build future African leaders.
🌍
Pan-African Network
Connect with young Africans from Kenya, Cameroon, Rwanda, Nigeria, Ghana and beyond — building lifelong friendships.
💼
Career Support
Get CV reviews, mock interviews, career guidance and public speaking training to boost your professional life.
🎓
Certificates
Earn official YAN certificates of participation, volunteer service and leadership — recognized across Africa.
💚
Mental Health Support
Access peer support, wellness sessions and a safe community that understands and supports your wellbeing journey.
Who Can Join

Membership Categories

🌟
Ordinary Member
Ages 18–35
Actively participate in all programs. Eligible to vote and hold leadership positions within YAN.
🔥
Volunteer Member
Ages 18–40
Join a department and contribute your skills to building YAN. Minimum 3-month commitment required.
🤝
Associate Member
All Ages
Organizations, mentors, researchers and allies who support YAN's mission and want to partner with us.
